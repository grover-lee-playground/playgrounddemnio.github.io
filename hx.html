HX - HAPPY EXPERIENCE

Project

leading companies know they have the same resources as technology, consultants, suppliers, market conditions and they know that the only way to differentiate is through a unique experience that connects emotionally with their customers.

Why was the project created?
It was created with the idea that people and organizations should create happy experiences with clients to create an emotionally strong bond with them. Otherwise, it would just be a transactional experience, like an ATM. And nobody has a bond with an ATM.

How is it achieved?
Monitoring and measuring permanently the emotional expressions of collaborators and clients. To maintain the desired experience and experiment what can create unique experiences.

How do I do it?
Through an artificial intelligence system that is installed on the computer used by the collaborators at their counters and two web cameras (one for the collaborator and one for the client). The system permanently monitors (24/7) the happiness of each and evaluates according to established metrics. These metrics are permanently sent to a database and processed to be able to visualize them and make decisions or give feedback.
